OUR DOMICILIARY CARE

DOMICILIARY CARE

Our Domiciliary Care Services offer a range of in-home care and support that meets your needs. The service is tailored to help you with your activities of daily needs.


Support will include:
  • Personal care support
  • Medication administration
  • Meal preparation
  • Light house work
  • Support with transfers
AFTER HOSPITAL CARE

Our After Hospital Care is support that is offered on a short-term basis to help you manage at home after being in hospital for a while. We support you during your recovery.


When to consider it:
  • After having surgery
  • Lost mobility / stroke
  • When prescribed bed rest
  • Increased falls risk
  • When feeling weak
SPECIALIST CARE

Our Specialist Care Services helps people living with physical, mental and chronic illnesses live a quality and independent lifestyle at home for longer. We manage your illness.


Our areas of speciality:
  • Diabetes management
  • Medication administration
  • Multiple sclerosis (MS)
  • Dementia & Alzheimers
  • Parkinsons
RESPITE CARE

Our Respite Care provides temporary relief for a primary caregiver, enabling them to take a much-needed break from the demands of their role as a caregiver.


Where this can happen:
  • In the individual's home
  • In a care/residential home
  • In a temp accommodation
  • In our respite living facility
  • On vacation
COMPANIONSHIP SERVICE

Our focus is on reducing loneliness and the impact of social isolation. We can chat, sip a cuppa with you and get out and about. We keep you company!


Things we can do:
  • Doing activities/going out
  • Escorting to coffee groups
  • Assisting with routines
  • Visiting friends or family
  • Be-friending
